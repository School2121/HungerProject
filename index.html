Facts About Hunger

Here are some powerful facts about global hunger:

  • 828 million people go to bed hungry every night.
  • Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger-related causes.
  • Malnutrition is responsible for nearly half of deaths in children under 5.
  • Women and children are most affected by starvation.

Causes of Hunger

Conflict, climate change, and poverty are the biggest drivers of world hunger. In many places, wars destroy food supply systems, while extreme weather makes farming harder than ever.

Ways You Can Help

  • Donate to hunger relief organizations.
  • Volunteer at your local food bank.
  • Spread awareness through social media.
  • Reduce your personal food waste.

Solutions for Ending Hunger

By reducing food waste, improving food distribution, and empowering local farmers, we can create a world without hunger. Policy changes, education, and sustainable practices are key!
